# Blog Content Refinement Template

Use this template when refining blog post ideas for content marketing.

## ChatGPT Prompt for Blog Ideas

```markdown
You are a content strategist and SEO expert specializing in blog content that drives business results. Help me transform this rough blog idea into a comprehensive content strategy.

My blog post idea: [PASTE YOUR IDEA HERE]

Please guide me through this analysis:

## CONTENT STRATEGY ANALYSIS

### 1. AUDIENCE & POSITIONING
- **Primary audience**: Who exactly should read this?
- **Secondary audience**: Who else might find value?
- **Reader intent**: What are they trying to accomplish?
- **Knowledge level**: Beginner, intermediate, or advanced?
- **Pain points addressed**: What specific problems does this solve?

### 2. SEO & DISCOVERABILITY
- **Primary keyword**: Main search term (high volume, relevant)
- **Secondary keywords**: 3-5 supporting terms
- **Long-tail opportunities**: Specific phrases with lower competition
- **Search intent**: Informational, commercial, or transactional
- **Content gap analysis**: What's missing from existing content?

### 3. CONTENT STRUCTURE
- **Compelling headline**: 3 options with power words
- **Meta description**: SEO-optimized snippet (150-160 chars)
- **Content outline**: H2 and H3 headings with key points
- **Content type**: How-to, listicle, case study, opinion piece
- **Content depth**: Word count recommendation (1000-5000+)

### 4. VALUE PROPOSITION
- **Unique angle**: What makes this different from existing content?
- **Expertise showcase**: How does this position us as thought leaders?
- **Actionable takeaways**: What will readers be able to do after reading?
- **Content upgrades**: Lead magnets or downloads to offer
- **Internal linking**: How does this connect to existing content?

### 5. ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY
- **Hook opening**: Compelling first paragraph approach
- **Visual elements**: Images, charts, screenshots needed
- **Interactive elements**: Polls, quizzes, calculators
- **Social sharing**: What quotes/stats are highly shareable?
- **Discussion starters**: Questions to drive comments

### 6. CONVERSION STRATEGY
- **Call-to-action**: Primary action we want readers to take
- **Lead generation**: How to capture reader information
- **Product mentions**: Natural ways to reference our solutions
- **Trust signals**: Testimonials, stats, case studies to include
- **Next content**: What should they read after this?

## EFFORT & RESOURCE ANALYSIS

### CONTENT CREATION EFFORT
- **Research time**: [X hours] - competitor analysis, data gathering
- **Writing time**: [X hours] - first draft creation
- **Design time**: [X hours] - visuals, formatting, graphics
- **Review/editing**: [X hours] - revisions and approvals
- **SEO optimization**: [X hours] - keyword optimization, meta tags
- **Total effort**: [X hours]

### REQUIRED RESOURCES
- **Subject matter expert**: Who needs to be interviewed?
- **Designer support**: What visual assets are needed?
- **Data/research**: What statistics or studies to include?
- **Tools needed**: Analytics, keyword tools, design software
- **External resources**: Photography, illustrations, data

### PROMOTION EFFORT
- **Social media**: [X hours] - creating posts across platforms
- **Email marketing**: [X hours] - newsletter inclusion, sequences
- **Distribution**: [X hours] - outreach, syndication, partnerships
- **Community engagement**: [X hours] - responding to comments
- **Repurposing**: [X hours] - creating derivative content

## SUCCESS METRICS

### PRIMARY METRICS
- **Organic traffic**: Target monthly search visits
- **Engagement rate**: Time on page, scroll depth, comments
- **Lead generation**: Form submissions, newsletter signups
- **Conversion rate**: Sales/demos attributed to this content
- **Search ranking**: Target position for primary keyword

### SECONDARY METRICS
- **Social shares**: Target shares across all platforms
- **Backlinks**: Number of sites linking to this content
- **Brand awareness**: Mentions, reach, impressions
- **Authority building**: Expert quotes, press mentions
- **Content velocity**: How this content enables future content

### SUCCESS TIMELINE
- **Week 1-2**: Initial traffic and engagement patterns
- **Month 1**: SEO ranking improvements, lead generation
- **Month 3**: Long-term organic traffic growth
- **Month 6**: Authority building and backlink development
- **Year 1**: Evergreen traffic and conversion performance

## CONTENT BRIEF OUTPUT

Generate a comprehensive content brief including:

**TITLE OPTIONS**: [3 compelling headlines with target keywords]

**CONTENT OUTLINE**: 
- Introduction Hook
- Main Section Headers (H2)
- Supporting Points (H3)
- Conclusion/CTA

**SEO SPECIFICATIONS**:
- Primary keyword: [keyword]
- Target word count: [number]
- Meta description: [160 chars]
- Image alt text suggestions

**CALL-TO-ACTION STRATEGY**: [Specific conversion goals and placement]

**PROMOTION PLAN**: [Distribution channels and timeline]

**SUCCESS CRITERIA**: [Measurable outcomes and KPIs]

---

Please analyze my blog idea and provide the complete content brief above. Ask clarifying questions if the initial idea is too vague.
```

## Usage Instructions

1. **Copy the template above**
2. **Replace `[PASTE YOUR IDEA HERE]`** with your actual blog concept
3. **Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini**
4. **Answer any clarifying questions** the AI asks
5. **Use the content brief** to create your blog post
6. **Track the success metrics** over time

## Example Input/Output

**Input**: "Write about AI tools for productivity"

**Output**: Complete content strategy including:
- Target audience: Mid-level professionals and developers
- Primary keyword: "AI productivity tools 2024"
- Content outline: 10 tools with specific use cases
- Success metrics: 5,000 monthly organic visits
- Conversion strategy: Free tool comparison guide download
